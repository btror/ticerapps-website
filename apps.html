Length Unit Converter

This is a simple app that allows you to convert one unit of length to another unit of length.

All you have to do is select a unit you want to convert from, enter an amount of said unit, select a unit to convert to, choose a decimal place, then press the convert button. The output will be displayed on the bottom tab. Each conversion will stay listed on the page until it they are cleared by pressing the "clear list" button.

Apportionment Calculator

This is a simple app that allows you to apportion seats to states using Hamilton's, Jefferson's, Webster's, or Adam's method of apportionment. Quotas and fair-shares are displayed.

All you have to do is select the apportionment method you want to use, enter an amount of seats to apportion, add as many states you want and enter their populations, then press the "calculate" button. There is a button to remove individual states and one to clear all.

Digital Storage Converter

All you have to do is select a unit you want to convert from, enter an amount of said unit, select a unit to convert to, then press the convert button. The output will be displayed on the bottom tab. Each conversion will stay listed on the page.
